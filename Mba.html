MBA

The P.G Department of Master of Business Administration (M.B.A) of GATE has been established in the year 2020. The department has been actively engaged in teaching and research in diverse fields of Computer Science and Application. With excellent experienced faculties, the department offers undergraduate (BBA) and Postgraduate (MBA) courses.

The students of this department has been placed in higher ranks in various Industries and Govt. sectors like and many more.

The department is also enriched with experienced and quality faculties. Along with their basic duties of teaching they also guide our students to carry out project works. The students are also mentored by professors from IITs and NITs to carry out their internship projects. The faculties also engage themselves in guiding the scholars from our own workspace as well as from other colleges and universities. The department provides a platform for the students as well as scholars from outside domain to pursue their carrier for future endeavor.

Mission

The mission of MBA Department is to empower the students to play a key role through the competency based educational and training programs that promote planned and sustainable growth of the society, state, country and the entire world with employable technical skills consistent with latest technological innovations. The aspiration is also to develop the student’s caliber in order to fulfill the challenging needs of industries expectations and to spring up the department to attain the international quality in the field of Research & Development. The undertaking is to provide high quality education in the field of MBA and in that way to create MBA professionals with proper leadership skills, commitment and moral values.

Vision

Our commitment to transparency and stress on equal opportunity has been a key contributing factor behind our emergence as an institute of national repute for better technocrats, rich in values and excellence, with a promising future and to be a pioneering institution in the field of education & technology to bring a new level of inspiration among the students.

Objectives

P. G. Department of Master of Business Adiministration (MBA) with an intake of finance Management 120 students, 60 students in General Management,IBM 120 students was established in the year 2020 under the affiliation of BPUT. The Primary objective of this program is to train top class MBA professionals so as to cater for the growing needs of various disciplines of MBA.

The main objective of the programme is to provide professional training in the area MBA Profr and to development to meet the demand of the fast growing IT industry who excel in their profession through innovative ideas, knowledge and team work. Course work is designed to reflect the state-of-the-art both in its theoretical and application dimensions. The department MBA students through class room lectures, seminars, group exercises, industry visits, projects and guest lectures by eminent personalities of the IT industry.
Our course curriculum is designed to provide our students a solid conceptual foundations and an extensive focus on application of these concepts to real life situations. The course content is a perfect blend of recent trends of MBA subjects in all Fourth semesters. The Fourth semester is dedicated exclusively for project work. The students undertake projects covering almost the entire array of MBA in various reputed industries in the country.
