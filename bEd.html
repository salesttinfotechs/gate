B.Ed Course

B.Ed course in an undergraduate program commonly known as Bachelors of Education. The duration of this course is generally about two years, which consists four semesters. This course is particularly opted by those who want to pursue their career in teaching and associated fields. This course can also be pursued through distance learning or correspondence which is usually about 2 years.

1st year of the correspondence course is bit prevalent for those who are not confirmed about the institute. Students from science stream are taught with the subjects like mathematics, chemistry, physics and biology. Whereas art stream students learn history, civics, economics, geography, political science and sociology. B.Ed is necessary for those who want to teach in primary schools and high schools. National council for teacher education is the constitutional body which regulates teaching courses in India.

Eligibility for B.Ed course in India

  • The minimum qualification required to pursue this course is Bachelor of science, Bachelor of commerce and Bachelor of arts from a reputed institute or university.
  • The Percentage required for this course-minimum 50%
  • DD (Demand Draft) Rs 20,000/ in favor of gate Group of Colleges Payable at Delhi.

The Benefits of pursuing B. Ed course:

  1. The aim of this course is to mold the teachers who are compassionate, creative towards the teaching profession and are guided by the principle of ethical and social responsibility towards the students.
  2. It develops our knowledge and skills to provide quality edification to the students.
  3. A Career in teaching gives you job satisfaction, high earnings, flexible timings and job security.
  4. After B. Ed candidates can pursue for M.Ed “ Masters in Education from several Indian Universities.

After the course you can apply for job in various places :

  • Government jobs
  • Education department
